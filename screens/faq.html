Evida is a UK preventive-health membership. We combine blood tests, wearable data, and GP-led consultations to create a personalised prevention plan. The Evida Protocol — Track, Tailor, Act — guides every member from baseline assessment through ongoing engagement. We're clinician-led, data-powered, and AI-assisted, but never replace your NHS GP.
Create an account, then book your Baseline: a Randox blood test at a clinic near you, followed by a 45-min virtual GP consultation. After your consult, you'll receive a personalised prevention plan with clinician-endorsed actions. You can connect wearables and upload records at any point.
The Evida Protocol is our three-step model: Track — measure your baseline through blood tests, wearables, and a health questionnaire. Tailor — a GP and health coach design a personalised prevention plan based on your results. Act — follow your plan, track progress, and check in with your care team.
No referral needed. Evida is a private membership — you can join directly. We'll share relevant health data with your NHS GP if you choose to authorise it, but there's no requirement.
Membership is £320 per year. For our pilot launch, the price is £160 — a 50% discount for early members. This includes your Baseline blood test, GP consultation, personalised plan, ongoing dashboard access, and two follow-up 15-min consults within 12 months.
Your membership includes: a comprehensive Randox blood test (42 biomarkers), a 45-min virtual GP consultation with a health coach, a personalised prevention plan with two reports, ongoing dashboard with wearable and biomarker tracking, two 15-min follow-up consults within 12 months, and access to Ask Evi — our non-diagnostic health assistant.
You can cancel at any time. If you cancel within 14 days of joining and before your consultation, you'll receive a full refund. After your consultation, membership is non-refundable but you keep access to your reports and plan. Appointment cancellations require 3 to 7 days' notice to account for fixed partner clinical fees — see our rescheduling policy below for details.
We require 3 to 7 days' notice for appointment cancellations or rescheduling. This window covers fixed clinical fees charged by our partner clinics and GPs. Cancellations made within 3 days of the appointment may incur a fee of up to 50% of the appointment cost. To reschedule or cancel, go to your Appointments tab, select the appointment, and choose the option. For urgent assistance, contact our care team.
Go to your dashboard and select "Membership Baseline" or "Book Baseline". You'll choose a Randox clinic location, pick a blood-test slot, then the system shows you the earliest available GP consultation slot (4–5 working days later — your results need ~72 hours to process). Review your cart, pay, and you're confirmed.
Your blood test results take up to 72 hours to process at the lab. Your GP needs your results before the consultation to give you a meaningful analysis and personalised plan. The system automatically blocks dates that are too early and explains why.
You can reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment. The blood test and GP consult are linked, so rescheduling one may affect the other — the system will show you available combinations. For urgent cancellations, contact our care team.
When you select your slots and reach the cart, those time slots are held for 15 minutes while you complete payment. If the timer runs out, the slots are released and you'll need to pick new ones. This ensures fair access for all members.
