### Membership Page Messaging

- “More than a health check” settled as the headline hook
- Subheadline options debated: “preventative health powered by data” vs. something around healthier years
  - “Your membership to healthier years” preferred direction
  - “Adding healthy years” also in the mix, same region of meaning
- Three-tier text structure questioned: could collapse to one strong line + list below
- Carousel layout flagged as a blocker for scannability
  - Dev was asked to fix it; not done yet
  - Critical info should be easily viewable, not scrolling

### Key Messaging Propositions

- Core value props agreed: convenience, trust, healthy years
- “More than a health check” peaks interest, then:
  - Ephemeral layer: quality of life, healthier years
  - Concrete layer: data, insight, action
    - Blood tests, wearables, medical history in one place
    - 90 mins GP consoles across the year
    - Follow-up support team
- Wearable integration flagged as the main differentiator and hook
  - Resonates strongly in conversation; should be more front and center
  - Caveat: still works without a wearable
- Lifestyle focus and GP time also strong differentiators
- Positioning: tech-enabled service, not an app
  - Human element (GP consoles) + tech element (wearables, data) both matter
  - Key message: “we are in service of our customers”
